The Skew Method

Structured.
Sequential.
Deliberate.

A structured approach to aligning governance, communication, and capability in complex government reform. Designed to create lasting change.

Why sequence matters

Why This Sequence Matters

Complex reform fails when organisations try to build capability without clear governance, or establish communication systems without defined decision rights. Our method follows a deliberate sequence that creates alignment from the foundation up.

1

Governance — Establish Structure

Phase 1 / Assess

We begin by establishing clear governance structures, decision rights, and accountability frameworks. This creates the foundation for informed decision-making and effective oversight.

Key Activities

  • Stakeholder mapping and engagement
  • Decision rights definition
  • Accountability framework design
  • Governance structure implementation

Expected Outcomes

  • Clear decision-making authority
  • Defined escalation pathways
  • Established oversight mechanisms
  • Documented governance model
↓
2

Communication — Enable Clarity

Phase 2 / Design

With governance in place, we design communication systems that ensure the right information reaches the right people at the right time. This enables transparency and informed decision-making.

Key Activities

  • Information flow mapping
  • Communication channel design
  • Reporting framework development
  • Stakeholder engagement protocols

Expected Outcomes

  • Transparent information flows
  • Effective reporting mechanisms
  • Informed stakeholder base
  • Reduced communication gaps
↓
3

Capability — Build and Sustain

Phase 3 / Sustain

Finally, we build the capabilities needed to execute reform and sustain change. This includes skills development, process implementation, and change management support.

Key Activities

  • Capability gap analysis
  • Training and development
  • Process design and implementation
  • Change management support

Expected Outcomes

  • Enhanced organisational capability
  • Sustainable operating models
  • Skilled workforce
  • Embedded change practices

Guiding Principles

Our method is guided by principles that ensure we deliver sustainable outcomes tailored to your organisation's needs.
